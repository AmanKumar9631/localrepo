Monitoring, Evaluation, and
Learning (MEL)

Monitoring, Evaluation, and Learning (MEL) is a comprehensive service offering where we combine cutting-edge quantitative and qualitative methods with a collaborative, adaptive framework to ensure faster and timely evidence-based program & policy  decision-making. Our domain experts apply a scientific lens to conduct assessments, and track performance of interventions. With deep cross-sectoral expertise, we also provide technical assistance and support to partners in developing monitoring and evaluation frameworks based on program theory of change, validation of performance indicators, data dashboards and analytics to enable focused and results-driven programming.

Research and evidence synthesis are vital for generating insights, identifying priorities, and shaping effective strategies in the social impact sector. They help organisations make informed, evidence-based decisions and allocate resources to what works. Our team not only produces original research but also synthesizes large bodies of evidence to uncover gaps and generate actionable insights. Our team also brings deep expertise in policy research, market research for social enterprises, and implementation science research.

Advisory - Programme
and Policy Science

Having demonstrated remarkable resilience through recent crises, nonprofits are reimagining their roles in a rapidly changing world. With limited time and resources, they are eager to adopt more strategic approaches that amplify their impact. Grounded in robust research and data analysis, we have made significant contributions in program and policy designs across the globe. This practice team supports in designing strategies and implementation plans along with facilitation of consultations with various stakeholders, including government bodies, civil society, and communities for inclusive program or policy formulation.

Projects

Many nonprofits operate with constrained budgets, limiting their ability to invest in advanced data systems, technologies, or specialised talent. As a result, they often rely on self-reported or external data sources, which can be inconsistent or incomplete. Integrating data from various platforms—such as surveys, CRM systems, and field reports—can also be technically challenging. We help social impact organisations unlock the full potential of their data, combining internal and publicly available sources to assess program success and scalability. Our support to clients include: descriptive analytics to identify trends and performance patterns, prescriptive analytics to guide strategic decisions, predictive analytics to forecast future outcomes, and scale for impact using tools like Social Return on Investment (SROI), cost-effectiveness analysis, and Results-Based Management (RBM). This comprehensive approach enables organisations to make informed, data-driven decisions and maximise their impact.

Knowledge Services

A centralised, easily accessible repository is key to any organisation’s success—and we help make that vision a reality. By organising meaningful evidence, actionable insights, toolkits, frameworks, reports, and best practices in one place, we create dynamic knowledge hubs that foster collaboration, innovation, and informed decision-making for greater social impact. In fast-changing environments, adaptive learning and strong knowledge management systems are essential to remain responsive and effective. Our services focus on building systems that support continuous learning, knowledge sharing, and real-time feedback integration to drive better outcomes. We also design intuitive, customisable dashboards that bring your data to life. By integrating inputs from diverse sources—surveys, CRM tools, field reports, financial systems—we deliver a unified view to support efficient project management, performance tracking, and strategic decision-making across your organisation.
